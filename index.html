Hey... Nice to meet you!

I'm a Front-End Web Developer based in Toronto. I have a Bachelor's degree in Media Arts, as well as recent training in front-end development from HackerYou College of Technology.

I previously grew up in rural Virginia in the States, and spent most of my adult life in Los Angeles running a TV production office. In LA I became inspired to code after a transformative experience working at an ultra-fast-paced tech startup.

After settling in Toronto, I'm full-steam ahead in pursuing my passion for building memorable user experiences that make the world a better place, one click at a time. Let's work together!
