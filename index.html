I'm a Front-End Web Developer based in Toronto. I was trained in development at HackerYou College of Technology, and I have a Bachelor's in Media Arts & Design with a focus on Corporate Communciation.

A transformative experience working at a tech start-up prompted me to make a big change. I'm now full-steam ahead in pursuing my passion for building memorable user experiences that make the world a better place, one click at a time.

I'm as adaptable as they come, and a genuine people person. Don't hestitate... let's chat!
